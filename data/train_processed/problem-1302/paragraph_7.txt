Reminder that the search warrant for Mar-a-Lago relied on potential offences under 18 USC 793(e), which criminalises anyone who "willfully retains [sensitive information] and fails to deliver it to the officer or employee of the United States entitled to receive it" [emphasis added], and offences under 18 USC 2071 about anyone who "willfully and unlawfully conceals, removes, mutilates, obliterates, falsifies, or destroys" government documents.
