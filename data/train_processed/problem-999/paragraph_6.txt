In terms of why the names are still secret: she went to court about a month ago to petition the judge to keep the names secret until she makes a charging decision. Her argument is that releasing the names recommended for indictment by the grand jury could violate the future defendant's civil rights (due process). In other words: they'd be accused of crimes without an opportunity to defend themselves. Her concern is that a due process violation could allow a defendant to get the indictment quashed, the venue changed, a conviction appealed, or otherwise escape going to prison.
