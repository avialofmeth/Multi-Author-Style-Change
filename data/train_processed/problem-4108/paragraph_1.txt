The infant mortality rate is higher in black communities. Black women receive poorer or no prenatal care on average and they also die more at childbirth. Black children live below the poverty line 3 times more than white ones. Unemployment rate is higher among black Americans. Redlining still affects 3/4 of redlined neighborhoods - all predominantly black. The networth of white households is 10 times greater than that of black ones, along with house ownership being almost 3 times higher. SAT scores and college admission are lower for black people. Black Americans are 5.6 times more likely to be arrested for the same offense. They are 1/3 of the inmate population despite being 12% of the population. Black people are killed by police officers more than twice as much as whites.
